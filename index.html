Sannie's Gym


It's a lifestyle

At Sannie's we live differently

Picture yourself at a gym right now, sweating it out against the uphill battle so apparent. It's tough to face that challenge every single day of your life. Here at Sannie's we're a community built gym with a great ambience and hard-working ethos. We care about our members and ensure that the user experience, is beyond expectation. To obtain a better lifestyle for yourself, is a daunting task. Join Sannie's today to be a part of a better, and fitter community; today.

It wasn't too long ago that we started off in a small space with barely any equipment, but we had a lot of heart and we built something we're proud of. We have many outreach activities, we love to educate the youth about the importance of health and fitness, keeping a lot of kids off the street and occupied. We've recently moved to a new premise, boasting facilities that could house over a thousand members at the same time, from a pool to top of the range cardio and weight lifting equipment. We've even got great expertise with personal trainers on hand, there to help you accomplish your goal.
